How does it work ?

A member publishes a meal, choosing the number of participants and their roles*.

* helping cook: You will help the host to cook.
  helping wash: You will help to order and clean.
  simple guest: You will help by paying a little bit more.

Others members subscribe, selecting the role they want to take.
Let’s enjoy, preparing together and eating together the meal.

* Helping cook: you will help the host to cook   Helping wash: you will help to order and clean   Simple guest: you don't help but pay a little bit more.

What about the money ?

The price of the groceries is divided between the participants.
  • If everybody helps: everybody pays the same.
  • If there are “simple guests”: they pay a little bit more and this surplus is shared between the host and the helpers.
    Don’t worry we make the calculations for you (see more).
You pay directly to the host who bought the groceries. THERE ARE NO WEBSITE FEES. Our service is completely free.

myCommuneaty : What is it ?

Meeting
Let’s meet gorgeous people who come from all over the world.
Sharing
Let’s share together the meal but also its preparation and the price of the groceries.
Conviviality
Let’s organize or participate to meals from three participants to many more.
Authenticity & Simplicity
Let’s enjoy homemade food prepared with love. All level of cookers are welcomed to organize meals.
Community
We will also organize events where all the members of myCommuneaty will be invited.
